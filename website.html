Incentive rebate/credit program

Economic incentives. October 8, 2018

Implement an incentive rebate/credit affinity program - each time you utilize a non-personal vehicle transportation option - you win points or receive some other credit incentive that could be redeemed in toll credits or other public admission credits (museums, performances, etc.)
